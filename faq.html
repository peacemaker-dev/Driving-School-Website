Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our driving lessons and services.

Do I need any documents to start lessons?

Yes. You'll need a valid South African ID or passport and proof of residence.

Can I book a lesson without a learner’s license?

You can book theory lessons, but you’ll need a learner’s license before practical driving can begin.

How do I make payments?

You can pay via cash, EFT, or using your preferred mobile banking service. Deposits are required before lessons start.

How long does a lesson last?

Lesson durations vary by course: beginner lessons are usually 30–60 minutes per session.

Can I hire a car for my test?

Yes, car/truck hire is available for your driving test, depending on the package you select.
